# Source policy

The live version is `/methodology`; keep this file aligned with it.

- Substantial claims receive stable C-IDs.
- Each claim must cite at least two source IDs from different publishers.
- Different publishers are necessary but not sufficient: the editor must check that the sources are not merely copying the same underlying report.
- Primary and official sources establish what an institution said, published or legally defined. They do not automatically settle contested interpretation.
- Reported interviews establish that those people expressed those views, not that a country agrees.
- AI output is not evidence and does not count as independent review.
- `reviewed` status requires at least one recorded external human reviewer.
