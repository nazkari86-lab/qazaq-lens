# Qazaq Lens scope

## Mission

Provide concise, sourced, shareable and correctable context about Kazakhstan for international audiences.

## Primary validation question

Will Kazakhstanis voluntarily share a Qazaq Lens explainer, and will international readers use it as context rather than dismissing it as promotional copy?

## Current v1 scope

- English mobile-first static site
- Nine public-beta explainers
- Pudding-style visual story for the nine-myth pattern
- public claim and source ledgers
- evidence dashboard and methodology
- search and topic filtering
- installable offline-capable PWA
- correction form stored in Cloudflare D1 when configured
- static Cloudflare Workers deployment through Wrangler
- RSS, sitemap, social previews and structured data
- automated content, function, build and internal-link QA

## Excluded until demand is demonstrated

- AI chat or automatic image scanning
- user accounts or social feed
- general news coverage
- automatic social monitoring
- native iOS/Android codebase
- paid advertising
- multilingual publication without a qualified reviewer for each language

## Editorial non-negotiables

- no claim that Kazakhstan is problem-free
- no claim to represent all Kazakhstanis
- no independent-review label without an actual human reviewer
- no substantial claim without two different publishers
- no silent material correction
- no source treated as authoritative outside the question it can establish
- no sensitive identity article marked reviewed without relevant additional expertise
